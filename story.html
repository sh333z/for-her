This is not a love this is our story .

We didn’t meet like people in movies.
No flowers. No smiles.
We met by fighting online.

Two strangers. Two egos.
Anger in words… but destiny was quietly smiling.

After the fight, something strange happened.
We didn’t block each other.
We talked.

Days turned into late-night texts, and one night…
she sang a song for me.

Her voice wasn’t just music.
It felt like comfort.
That was the moment my heart betrayed me—
I got a crush on her without permission.

I waited.
Seven long months.

Every day I liked her more, feared losing her more.
And one day, I finally said it…
“I love you.”

She didn’t answer immediately.
But when she did…
she said yes.

That “yes” changed my life.

The first time I met her in real life,
I travelled 200 kilometers at night,
with a heart racing faster than the bus.

I entered her house quietly…
and then I saw her.

Her eyes.
Her hair.
Her smell.
Everything felt unreal—like she stepped out of my prayers.

She came closer…
and kissed me on my lips.

That was our first touch.
My world stopped breathing for a second.

We started meeting once a month,
counting days like children waiting for festivals.

One night at 2 o’clock,
she came with me on my bike.

I kissed her neck gently—
not out of desire,
but out of love and pride.

Then life tested us.

no messages.
No calls.
Only memories.

Then one day…
a notification.
Her name.

Today, it’s been four years.

We didn’t have a perfect story—
we had a real one.
